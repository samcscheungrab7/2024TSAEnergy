Most Common Fossil Fuels



  • Coal- Coal takes millions of years to create. It is made of sedimentary rock and old plants that got compressed over time. It mastly releases carbon into the air when it is burned for energy.





  • Oil- Oil is found in larger quantities under the Earth's surface in underground areas called reservoirs. It is mostly used for fuel or raw material in the chemical industry.





  • Natural Gas- Natural gas is a different type of fossil fuel. While coal and oil is made from prehistoric plants and animals from long ago natural gas is made when organic matter decays over a long time. We use it for heating, cooking, and power generation.
